My Course Feedback

The Course has 2 sections Tailwind Basics and Project section which is good and well structured

The First section goes around most of the Tailwind Basics Classes and Configurations I liked that section alot.

The projects section includes two types: Mini Projects (component designs) and Big Projects (full landing page designs).

The projects are well-executed, featuring advanced elements such as plugins, custom colors and backgrounds, custom classes, fonts, and appealing UI design.

Brad writes the code without re-explaining because he has covered everything before, so it's easy to end up just copying and pasting if you're not focused.

My advice to get the most out of this course is doing each task 3 times, one before you see how he did it, and one with him doing the project, and then the last time after you see him doing it
